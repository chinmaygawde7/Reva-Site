Services

The main services that we provide are

Development in Batch quanitity

Our design team design the drawing define tolerances and metallurgy for any machine parts ​ We can submit sample for approval before production.

Part Manufacturing

Manufacturing is outsourced to one of our supplier selected and validate as per standard

Quality

100% parts are inspected in our premises before assembly or dispatches to ensure a full compliance to the drawing.

Assembly

Parts are assembled in our premises as per the procedure. ​ They are tested as per the industry requiremnet before delivery to the customer
